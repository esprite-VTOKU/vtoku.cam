Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026 · Applies to VRL Cam (com.vtoku.cam)

The short version: VRL Cam does not collect, store, sell, or transmit any personal data to VTOKU for its own use. There are no accounts, no analytics, no ads, and no third-party tracking. Almost everything the app does happens on your device or on your own local network. The one exception is VRL Link — an optional feature you turn on that relays a live stream through our authorization server and a third-party real-time service; it carries only the stream you choose to send, and only while you have it running (see section 6).

1. Who we are

VRL Cam ("the app") is developed and published by VTOKU LLC ("VTOKU", "we", "us"), based in Honolulu, Hawai‘i, United States. This policy explains how the app handles information. You can reach us at support@vtoku.com.

2. Data we collect

None for our own use. The app contains no analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, crash-reporting services, or tracking technologies, and we build no profile of you. Apple's "App Tracking Transparency" does not apply because the app does not track you across other companies' apps or websites, and the app's bundled privacy manifest declares no tracking domains.

We operate one server — the VRL Link authorization service — and it is used only when you turn VRL Link on. It receives a random per-install identifier the app generates (not tied to your name, Apple ID, or device serial) and Apple's signed proof that the app is a genuine install, solely to issue a short-lived access credential for the live-streaming session and to rate-limit abuse. We do not use this to identify you, and it is not sold or shared for advertising. See section 6 for the full picture.

3. How your content and device data are used (on-device only)

To do its job, the app accesses the following on your device. This information is processed locally and in real time; it is not uploaded to us.

4. Avatars and recordings are yours

VRM avatar files you import, motion data you receive, and video takes you record are stored locally on your device and remain under your control. We have no access to them. Deleting the app, or removing files through the Files and Photos apps, removes that content from your device.

5. Settings

The app saves your preferences (such as connection addresses, ports, and toggle states) in the device's standard local settings store so they persist between launches. These settings stay on your device and are not sent to us.

6. Network streaming, explained

On your local network. VRL Cam is a tool for sending camera and avatar data to other software you run. When you enable a sender (VMC/OSC, FreeD, NDI) or discovery (NDI, iFacialMocap), the app communicates with devices on your local network using the addresses you specify or that are discovered on your LAN. We never see this traffic. What the receiving software (for example Warudo) does with the data is governed by that software's own privacy practices, not ours.

Over the internet (VRL Link and VDO.Ninja). VRL Link is an optional feature that lets you send your camera and avatar to a browser or receiver that isn't on your local network. To make that work, two things happen, and only after you turn the feature on:

  • Authorization. The app contacts our authorization server (operated by VTOKU on Fly.io) to obtain a short-lived access token for the session. The request includes a random per-install identifier the app generates and Apple's signed proof that the app is genuine. We use these only to issue the token and to rate-limit abuse; server logs store only a salted, one-way hash — never your raw IP address or a reversible identifier — and are not used to build a profile of you or shared for advertising.
  • The stream itself. Your live video, audio, and avatar data flow through a third-party real-time media service (LiveKit) that relays them to the people you share your room link with. This is a live relay: the media is not recorded or retained on the relay after the session, and we do not have access to its contents. LiveKit's handling of the transit is governed by its own privacy policy. VDO.Ninja (WHIP) works the same way through its real-time service.

If you never enable VRL Link or VDO.Ninja, the app never contacts our authorization server or any streaming relay, and none of your data leaves your device or local network by these paths.

7. Children's privacy

The app is rated 4+ and is suitable for general audiences. Because it collects no personal data, it collects none from children. It is not directed at children for the purpose of data collection.

8. Purchases

VRL Cam offers optional in-app purchases. Purchases are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. VTOKU does not process payments and does not receive your name, card, or billing details. Apple's handling of your purchase is governed by Apple's privacy policy. We only see anonymous, aggregate sales information that Apple reports to all developers, which does not identify you.

9. Third-party software

The app includes third-party libraries (for example the NDI SDK and open-source components) that run entirely on-device as part of the app and do not phone home to us. The optional VRL Link and VDO.Ninja streaming features additionally use a third-party real-time media service (LiveKit) that carries your stream only while you have streaming turned on, as described in section 6. Trademarks such as Warudo, NDI, VMC, VRM, and LiveKit belong to their respective owners; their inclusion does not imply endorsement. See Acknowledgements.

10. Your rights

Because we do not collect or hold any personal data about you, there is no personal data for us to access, correct, export, or delete on your behalf. You remain in full control of the content on your own device. If you have questions about your rights under laws such as the GDPR or CCPA, contact us and we will help.

11. Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page and post the revised version here. Material changes will be reflected in a future app update's notes where appropriate.

12. Contact

Questions about this policy or the app's privacy practices? Email support@vtoku.com or use our contact form.
