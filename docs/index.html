VRL Link

Link a remote performer: receive their motion and voice, send your camera and video back.

Default ports. VMC/OSC send 19190 · VMC receive from Warudo 39539 · FreeD 40000 (subnet broadcast). All of it stays on your local network.

Also built in. The avatar can be hidden behind real-world objects, defocused like a real subject, and graded with the Screen FX anime look. See Look & camera. VTOKU Cam also sends and receives face capture over iFacialMocap, and live streams over SRT with SRTLA link bonding. An Outdoor mode keeps tracking steady outdoors and in large spaces. Everything here is optional and off by default.
