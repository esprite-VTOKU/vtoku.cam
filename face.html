Set up in the app

  1. In VTOKU Cam open Settings, then VRL Link. Generate a room secret and turn on Connect.
  2. Copy the secret to this page. The room only accepts senders while the app is connected.
  3. In the Face panel set Source to VMC. Your avatar now follows this webcam.

Perfect sync sends all 52 ARKit blendshapes for avatars rigged for them. Leave it off for standard VRM models. Recenter captures your current head pose as neutral.

Privacy. Face tracking runs on your device with MediaPipe. Video and images are never uploaded. The page sends only VMC blendshape and head rotation values, end to end inside your private room. Works in current Chrome, Edge, and Safari on a device with a camera.
