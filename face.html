Set up in the app

  1. In VTOKU Cam open Settings, then VRL Link. Generate a room secret and turn on Connect.
  2. Copy the secret here and press Join. Joining only works while the app is connected.
  3. In the Face panel set Source to VMC. Your avatar now follows this webcam.

Privacy and options

Face tracking runs on your device with MediaPipe. Video and images are never uploaded; the page sends only VMC blendshape and head rotation values inside your private room.

Perfect sync is for avatars rigged with ARKit blendshapes; leave it off for standard VRM models. Recenter captures your current head pose as neutral. Works in current Chrome, Edge, and Safari.
