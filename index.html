Perform in the real world

Bring your avatar off the screen and into the room, ready to film or go live.

Use your phone as a virtual camera in Warudo

Walk, tilt, and push in. Your phone becomes a moving camera inside your Warudo scene, mirroring how you move in the real world.

Your VTuber, unleashed into the world

Bring any VRM model and step your character out of the screen and into the world in AR. Stream anywhere.

Built-in face tracking

Drive your avatar's expressions live with the front camera, or bring in external tracking like iFacialMocap.

Move and dance

Bring your avatar to life with built-in idle and dance moves, or load your own.

Record your performance

Capture your full performance in one take, ready to post or drop into your edit.

Collab with IRL streamers

Your avatar lives in real space, so VTubers and IRL streamers can share the same scene and stream together.

Bonded live streaming

Stream over SRT and bond Wi-Fi with cellular using SRTLA, so your feed holds up on the move (you'll need an SRTLA server). Backed by Moblin's open-source bonding tech. See the setup guide.

Connect with Warudo

The virtual camera tracking works with Warudo. Send your camera move and avatar into your scene over the network, and drive your avatar back onto your phone. Grab the Workshop plugin and follow the setup guide.

Monitor over HDMI

Output to a display or capture device over HDMI to monitor your shot on the big screen, or feed it into your production setup.
