VR meets IRL. Use your phone as a virtual camera, perform as your VTuber in AR, and take the show live anywhere.

Made to perform

Shoot, perform, and go live as your virtual self, all from the phone in your hand.

Move the camera with your phone

Walk around, tilt, and push in. Your phone is the camera in your scene, so every move you make lands in the shot.

Your VTuber, unleashed into the world

Bring any VRM model, step your character out of the screen and into real space in AR, and perform as them anywhere.

Act with your face

Smile, blink, and talk, and your avatar does it with you, live from the front camera.

Move and dance

Bring your avatar to life with built-in idle and dance moves, or load your own.

Record your performance

Capture your full performance in one take, ready to post or drop into your edit.

Go live anywhere

Stream live even on the move. Bond Wi-Fi and cellular together so your feed holds up out in the real world.

Works on its own

No PC, no extra gear. Your phone is the whole rig: track, perform, record, and go live, all from one device.

Connect with Warudo

Send your camera and avatar straight into Warudo over your network, and bring its motion back onto your phone. Grab the Workshop plugin and follow the setup guide.

On device

Built for iPhone and iPad, with a camera-style interface that feels right from the first shot.
