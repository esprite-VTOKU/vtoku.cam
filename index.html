What you can do

Shoot, perform, and go live as your virtual self, all from the phone in your hand.

Move the camera with your phone

Walk around, tilt, and push in. Your phone is the camera in your scene, so every move you make lands in the shot.

Become your VTuber

Bring your character into the room in AR and perform as them. Sample avatars are built in, so you can start the moment you open the app.

Act with your face

Smile, blink, and talk, and your avatar does it with you, live from the front camera.

Move and dance

Bring your avatar to life with built-in idle and dance moves, or load your own.

Record your performance

Capture video with sound straight to your camera roll, ready to post or edit. Your orientation stays locked so a mid-shot rotation can't ruin the take.

Go live anywhere

Stream live even on the move. Bond Wi-Fi and cellular together so your feed holds up out in the real world.

Start in seconds

No extra gear, no setup. Open the app, pick an avatar, and you're rolling. Record and it saves straight to your camera roll.

Fits your studio

Already run Warudo or an NDI setup? VTOKU Cam drops right in: send your camera move and avatar into your scene and trade video over your network. All optional. See the docs.

On device

Built for iPhone and iPad, with a camera-style interface that feels right from the first shot.

Your footage stays yours

Camera, microphone, and face data are processed on device. Recordings save to your own Photos library, and the streaming features send data only to receivers and servers you choose. VTOKU collects nothing. Read the privacy policy.
